About

Advocacy for the Unhoused is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and student-led initiative dedicated to supporting unhoused individuals across Massachusetts by donating supplies and building awareness about the problem. Founded in 2025 as a small school club, we have grown into a federally-recognized organization with multiple branches and a team of over 50 committed volunteers who believe that housing should be accessible to everyone.

We recognize that homelessness is rarely the result of a single event. Rising rent, inflation, medical bills, and the general cost of living continue to climb while wages remain stagnant. Many people are pushed into impossible situations where even working multiple jobs is not enough to stay housed. Our work is grounded in the belief that no one should face these pressures alone, and that communities have a responsibility to support those most affected by economic instability.

In our first year, we raised more than $1,000, which we used to buy essential supplies such as blankets, toiletries, and hygiene products. Every dollar we raise goes directly toward purchasing these items. All operational costs are covered separately, allowing us to maintain complete financial transparency.
