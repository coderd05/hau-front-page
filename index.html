About

Advocacy for the Unhoused is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and student-led initiative dedicated to supporting unhoused individuals across Massachusetts by donating supplies and building awareness about the problem. Founded in 2025 as a small school club, we have grown into a federally-recognized organization with multiple branches and a team of over 50 committed volunteers who believe that housing should be accessible to everyone.

In our first year, we raised more than $1,000, which we used to buy essential supplies such as blankets, toiletries, and hygiene products. Every dollar we raise goes directly toward purchasing these items.
