Advocacy for the Unhoused, Inc.

Advocacy | Awareness | Action

Advocacy for the Unhoused is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and student-led initiative dedicated to supporting unhoused individuals across Massachusetts. Founded in 2025 as a small school club, we have grown into a multi-branch organization with over 50 committed volunteers.

In our first year, we raised more than $1,000, used entirely for essential supplies. We believe in 100% financial transparency—every dollar you give is tracked and reported.

Our mission is simple: bring people together to support those who need it most, while empowering the next generation of leaders to take action.
